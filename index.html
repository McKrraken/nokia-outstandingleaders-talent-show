Celebration of Talent

Our mission is to celebrate the diversity and creativity within the LGBTQ+ community and our allies. This platform offers each of you a safe place to showcase what makes you special in a sea of sameness. Whether that is belting our a melody like Lady Gaga herself, Dancing like Bob Fosse, or any other talent you might want to showcase, this is your time to shine! ​
NOKIA is PROUD to partner together with other leaders in the industry to demonstrate our renewed commitment to the value of Diversity in the community. On this site you will find some interesting stories about the 2024 Outstanding Leader cohort. Scan the QR codes to find out more details. By leveraging our connections with partner organizations and allies, we have created a single, interactive platform to make your talents known! ​
