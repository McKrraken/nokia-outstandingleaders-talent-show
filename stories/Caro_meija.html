Carolina Mejia Lopez


At the beginning of this program I participated as a speaker in the LGBT... Who? event that was held on the last day in Finland.
It was the first time I stepped on a stage and shared my own story (I know, very meta of me telling my story by telling my story). The feedback I got from all of you swelled my little hamster heart.
I got to relieve that recently as the editing gnomes did their job and I shared the video amongst my peers. People still reach out to me to thank me for sharing my story.
I never would have imagined that I would take a stage and inspire people by being me. Showing strangers who I am and being encouraged and supported to do so.
I feel valued for who I am and what I look like, not in spite of who I am and what I look like (quote inspired by Ivan Coyote). I am experiencing queer joy at work and I get to share that with people like me. I am just... so happy I can be me.
