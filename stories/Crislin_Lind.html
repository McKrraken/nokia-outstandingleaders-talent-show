Crislin Lind


The Country Manager of Nokia Belgium turned to me one day and asked if I could start initiating and leading the LGBT+ community in the office. It was an absolute honor and full of excitement moment.
I gathered together more people in the office, who I knew that were involved in the community in general as well, and we hit it off! We created a handful of great events in the office, got more people to join the LGBT+ community and shared great memories.
We all went together to the Antwerpen Pride held in August 2024, and the Antwerpen office joined Open@Work Belgium since this year as well!
