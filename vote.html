Mia Petrasovic

“Like That” by Veiled Revolt is a relentless, politically charged anthem that explodes with raw aggression and chaotic energy. The track opens immediately with a grungy guitar riff. The vocals are intense, seething with frustration and defiance, delivering sharp, biting lyrics that tackle themes of corruption, inequality, and resistance.
The chorus is anthemic and cathartic, urging listeners to stand up and fight back. With its heavy, driving bass lines, explosive drums, and gritty production, the song feels like a battle cry, capturing the volatility and urgency of rebellion. The breakdown features a frenetic, almost disorienting guitar riff, amplifying the song’s chaotic energy before launching back into the full force of the chorus.
“Like That” leaves no room for subtlety—it’s a sonic punch to the gut, a furious call to action that doesn’t let up until the final note.
